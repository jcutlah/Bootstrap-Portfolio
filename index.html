About James

James Cutler has worked for 3+ years at HubSpot, currently as a Website Technical Consultant on their Website Migrations team. In his role, he works with HTML/CSS/JS and HubL (a Jinja-based language specific to HubSpot.) He also consults with Migrations customers and provides DNS/go-live assistance. In his spare time, he has become proficient in Python and REST API usage. Using Google Cloud Platform to host his Python applications, he has been able to permanently solve a number of business challenges that were faced by his team at HubSpot.

Inspired by his experience thus far, James has enrolled in the UNH Coding Bootcamp to further his web development learning. Here are the primary goals he has for this course:

  • Develop code management best practices using Git/GitHub.
  • Learn the ins and outs of hosting web and mobile apps.
  • Expanded JavaScript tool set.
  • Connect with Seacoast-area people in the tech industry or in technical roles.

While his current role is solely focused on front-end web development, he is on the lookout for a role that also includes server-side development.

James lives in Kittery, Maine with his wife, Katie, their son, Elwyn, and their dog, Toby. When he's able to break away from work and family, he likes to surf the southern ME/NH seacoast, or do something in the mountains (hike, climb, ski, etc.)
